# WorkforceIQ Workflow Guide

## Introduction

This document explains how WorkforceIQ operates inside a real organisation.

Unlike traditional HR systems, WorkforceIQ is not designed to manage employees.

It is designed to help employees grow, help managers develop talent, and help organisations make better workforce decisions.

The platform is built around four principles:

1. Evidence before opinion
2. Growth before judgement
3. Fairness before promotion
4. Development before replacement

Everything within WorkforceIQ follows these principles.

---

# The WorkforceIQ Philosophy

Most HR systems ask:

> What happened last year?

WorkforceIQ asks:

> What can this person become next?

Traditional systems focus on administration.

WorkforceIQ focuses on development.

Traditional systems record history.

WorkforceIQ helps shape the future.

# Employee Journey

## Afzal Ahmed

Senior Software Developer

Let's imagine Afzal joins the organisation.

---

# Week One

Afzal logs into WorkforceIQ.

Unlike a traditional HR system, he is not immediately asked to score himself.

No:

```text
Rate yourself 1-10
```

No:

```text
How good are you at leadership?
```

No:

```text
How good are you at communication?
```

Because self-assessment is unreliable.

Instead the platform asks for evidence.

Examples:

### Projects

Which projects have you worked on?

---

### Achievements

What are you proud of?

---

### Certifications

Which qualifications have you completed?

---

### Responsibilities

Have you mentored anyone?

Have you led projects?

Have you managed stakeholders?

---

### Career Interests

What interests you?

Examples:

* Leadership
* Architecture
* Product Development
* Management

Notice the difference.

Afzal is not scoring himself.

He is describing reality.
